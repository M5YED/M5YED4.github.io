Company Info

AssetFlow Automations was founded in October 2024(10/24). The company specialises in creating automation software programs for business and customer use. AssetFlow Automations is a business-neutral B2B business model, meaning that it creates systems that are tailored to its client's business/sector. The company creates systems that are custom-built.

About AssetFlow Automations

AssetFlow Automations specialises in building personalised automations tailored to improving the efficiency and productivity of businesses. The systems it creates are designed to integrate into existing business processes. Every system is tested and trialed for durability, reliability, and security. AssetFlow Automations began by creating documentation systems specifically for asset/investment management companies. Since the launch of "Project Eco", it has shifted to creating automation systems, including documentation, for all types of businesses(ie. business-neutral).

Project Eco

Project Eco is an initiative to provide businesses with systems that advance their business processes. The word "Eco" is short for "ecosystem", implying that systems provided by AssetFlow Automations are built to fit in with other systems that the business uses.

The Mission

Through the Project Eco initiative, AssetFlow Automations aims to provide businesses with robust automation ecosystems that adapt to their needs, regardless of industry. The main focus is on empowering organisations to achieve greater efficiency and innovation through bespoke automation solutions. The purpose of the initiative is to help prepare businesses for the future of business in an AI-evolving landscape.

AFA

Agent AFA is a sophisticated automation system developed by AssetFlow Automations, designed to streamline business operations and improve customer interactions through a combination of task-specific automation and advanced Large Language Models (LLM). Built to handle a range of business functions, AFA integrates LLM's(such as Claude and GPT) for tasks requiring advanced natural language processing(NLP) and intelligent decision-making. Capabilities of AFA include(but are not limited to): answering questions, detecting and handling extreme user emotion, sending emails, gathering market research and collecting feedback.
